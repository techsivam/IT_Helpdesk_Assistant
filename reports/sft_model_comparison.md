## Key Takeaways

The SFT model is vastly superior to the base model. It is:
1. **Domain and Company Aware**: It knows corporate URLs, support emails, phone numbers, and standard operating procedures (SOPs).
2. **Policy Enforcing**: It guides users to follow safety protocols (e.g., Software Center, reporting phishing, MFA verification) instead of suggesting unsafe workarounds.
3. **Structured and Professional**: The responses follow a standard helpdesk structure and are helpful and action-oriented.
