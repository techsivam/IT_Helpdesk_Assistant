## Evaluation Metrics

* **Correctness**: SFT and DPO are both highly correct. Base model is incorrect in policy matters.
* **Clarity**: DPO has the highest clarity. It avoids wordiness and breaks instructions down into logical steps.
* **Safety**: DPO enforces security rules (e.g. reporting phishing, Software Center compliance, MFA checks) with high urgency and professional authority.
* **Helpfulness**: DPO model answers are direct and provide URLs, phone numbers, and emails immediately, minimizing time-to-resolution.

## DPO Impact

The transition from SFT to DPO resulted in:
1. **Conciseness**: Reduced length by ~15-20% by stripping out verbose greetings and repetitive phrases.
2. **Professional Tone**: Structured as direct action plans for the user.
3. **Safety Alignment**: Explicitly flagged risky behavior (like manual software downloads or sharing credentials) and prioritized reporting mechanisms.
